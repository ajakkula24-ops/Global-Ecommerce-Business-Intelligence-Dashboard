# 🌍 Global E-Commerce Business Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)
Global e-commerce business intelligence dashboard built in Power BI, analyzing sales, profit, and geographical performance across countries, markets, and categories.

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end **Global E-Commerce Business Intelligence solution** built using **Power BI**.  
It analyzes worldwide sales performance across **countries, states, markets, categories, and sub-categories**, helping stakeholders make **data-driven decisions**.

The dashboard is designed in a **real-world corporate reporting style**, focusing on clarity, interactivity, and business insights.

📊 Analysis Report – Global E-Commerce Sales

(Power BI Business Intelligence Project)

1️⃣ Executive Summary

This project analyzes global e-commerce sales performance using transactional data across 147 countries and 1,094 states.
The objective is to identify revenue drivers, regional performance patterns, category trends, and optimization opportunities to support data-driven business decisions.

The analysis is delivered through an interactive Power BI dashboard covering Sales, Profit, Cost, Market distribution, Geography, and Product categories.

2️⃣ Overall Business Performance
Metric	Value
Total Sales	$12.64M
Total Profit	$1.47M
Total Cost	$11.18M
Profit Margin	~11.6%

Insight:
The business operates profitably with a moderate margin, indicating scope for cost optimization, pricing strategy improvements, and efficiency gains.

3️⃣ Market-Level Analysis

Sales contribution by market:

APAC – Highest contributor (~28%)

EU & US – Strong and consistent performance

LATAM & EMEA – Moderate growth markets

Africa & Canada – Lower contribution with expansion potential

Insight:
APAC drives the largest share of revenue, suggesting strong demand and scalability. Emerging markets present long-term growth opportunities.

4️⃣ Country-Level Performance

Top performing countries:

United States – ~35% of total sales

Australia

France

China

Germany

Insight:
Revenue is concentrated in a few developed economies, creating market dependence risk that can be reduced through geographical diversification.

5️⃣ State-Level Sales Analysis

Leading states:

England – ~38%

California – ~36%

Île-de-France – ~25%

Insight:
High sales concentration in major economic regions highlights the impact of logistics efficiency, customer density, and purchasing power.

6️⃣ Category Performance Analysis
Category	Sales
Technology	~$4.7M
Furniture	~$4.1M
Office Supplies	~$3.8M

Insight:
Technology leads due to higher unit value, while Furniture and Office Supplies provide stable, recurring revenue, supporting overall business stability.

7️⃣ Sub-Category Trend Analysis

Top sub-categories:

Phones

Copiers

Chairs

Storage

Low-performing sub-categories:

Labels

Fasteners

Envelopes

Insight:
A long-tail revenue pattern exists where a small number of products drive most sales. Low performers may require pricing review, bundling, or rationalization.

8️⃣ Geographical Insights

Sales are concentrated in North America, Europe, and APAC

Africa and parts of South America show low penetration

Insight:
Underpenetrated regions represent opportunities for targeted market entry, partnerships, and localized strategies.

9️⃣ Key Business Recommendations

📈 Revenue Growth

Expand high-performing Technology products in emerging markets

Increase marketing focus in APAC and EU

💰 Cost Optimization

Review logistics and procurement costs

Optimize low-margin sub-categories

🌍 Market Expansion

Pilot campaigns in Africa and LATAM

Introduce region-specific product strategies

📦 Product Strategy

Bundle low-performing products

Prioritize inventory for top revenue drivers

🔚 Conclusion

This Power BI dashboard provides a decision-ready, global view of e-commerce performance.
The analysis highlights strong core markets, profitable categories, and clear opportunities for expansion, optimization, and strategic growth.
